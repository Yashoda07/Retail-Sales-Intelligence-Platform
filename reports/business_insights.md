## Question 2: Which product category generates the highest sales?

### Findings


| Category | Sales | Profit |
|----------|-------:|-------:|
| Technology | $836,154.03 | $145,454.95 |
| Furniture | $741,999.80 | $18,451.27 |
| Office Supplies | $719,047.03 | $122,490.80 |

- Technology is the highest-selling category.
- Furniture is the second highest.
- Office Supplies is the third highest.

### Business Interpretation

Technology products contribute the largest share of revenue and should remain a strategic business focus. Furniture also contributes significantly, while Office Supplies generates comparatively lower sales. Further analysis should determine whether lower sales are due to lower demand or fewer products.

- Technology is the highest-performing category in both sales and profit.
- Furniture generates strong sales but contributes very little profit compared to the other categories.
- Office Supplies generates lower sales than Furniture but produces much higher profit.

### Business Recommendation

- Continue investing in Technology products as they generate both high revenue and high profit.
- Investigate why Furniture has low profitability despite strong sales. Possible reasons include high discounts, shipping costs, or procurement costs.
- Increase focus on Office Supplies, as it appears to be a highly profitable category.

## Question 3: Which sub-categories are losing money?

### Findings

| Sub-Category | Sales | Profit |
|--------------|-------:|--------:|
| Tables | $206,965.53 | **-$17,725.48** |
| Bookcases | $114,880.00 | **-$3,472.56** |
| Supplies | $46,673.54 | **-$1,189.10** |

### Business Interpretation

- **Tables** are the biggest concern. Despite generating **$206,965.53** in sales, they incur a loss of **$17,725.48**.
- **Bookcases** and **Supplies** are also operating at a loss.
- High sales do **not** necessarily translate into high profitability.
- The negative profits may be driven by:
  - Excessive discounts
  - High shipping costs
  - Low product margins

### Business Recommendations

- Review the **pricing and discount strategy** for the **Tables** sub-category.
- Analyze **shipping**, **procurement**, and **operational costs** for loss-making products.
- Prioritize investment in consistently profitable sub-categories such as:
  - Copiers
  - Phones
  - Accessories
  - Paper
- Implement automated alerts to identify products with **high sales but negative profit**, enabling faster corrective action.
