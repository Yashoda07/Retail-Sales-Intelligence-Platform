EDA & Business Analytics

Q1. What is the difference between Sales and Profit?

Sales is total revenue generated. Profit is the amount remaining after costs.

Q2. Why can a product have high sales but low profit?

Because of high discounts, high shipping costs, or low profit margins.

Q3. Which category performed best in your analysis?

Technology generated the highest sales and the highest profit.

Q4. Which sub-category was the biggest loss-maker?

Tables, with about -$17.7k profit despite high sales.

Q5. Why is identifying loss-making products important?

It helps businesses improve pricing, reduce costs, and avoid selling products that hurt overall profitability.
