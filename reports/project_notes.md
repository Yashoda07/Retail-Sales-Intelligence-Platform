# Retail Sales Intelligence Platform

## Project Objective

To analyze historical retail sales data and build an end-to-end Retail Sales Intelligence Platform that helps businesses understand sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional trends using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

---

## Dataset Source

Global Superstore Dataset

---

## Business Problem

A global retail company has years of sales data but lacks meaningful insights to support business decisions.

The company wants to:
- Identify top and bottom performing products.
- Analyze sales and profit trends.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Compare performance across regions and markets.
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on profit.
- Improve decision-making using data-driven insights.

---

## Project Scope

The project will include:

- Data understanding
- Data cleaning using Python
- Feature engineering
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- SQL-based business analysis
- Interactive Power BI dashboards
- Business insights and recommendations
- Sales forecasting
